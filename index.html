The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future generation.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Poverty is not created by the poor. It is the result of the systems, institutions, and policies that have been designed and maintained by society. The poor are full of potential, creativity, and ambition, yet they are held back by structures that limit their opportunities and access to resources. To eradicate poverty, we must reimagine these systems, fostering a world built on justice, equity, and opportunity for all. Only by transforming our approach can we truly create a world free from poverty and unlock the true potential of humanity."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
